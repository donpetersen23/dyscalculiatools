Building Solutions Together for Dyscalculia

Dyscalculia affects 3-7% of students - as common as dyslexia - yet it's rarely recognized or understood. Many bright kids struggle with math basics while their needs go unmet because teachers and parents don't know what dyscalculia looks like.

The challenge: Unlike dyslexia, dyscalculia lacks awareness, research funding, and proven interventions. Students often feel "bad at math" when they actually have a learning difference that can be supported.

Join our growing community: Whether you're discovering strategies in your classroom, researching new approaches, or supporting a child at home - your experience matters in changing this.

Together we can: Raise awareness, share proven techniques, and build the resources that students with dyscalculia truly deserve.

Every insight shared helps another student succeed and brings dyscalculia out of the shadows.
